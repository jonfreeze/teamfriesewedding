Our Story

Our love story is one of reconnecting after 20 years. Our parents belonged to a support group called L.A.P.A (Latin American Parent's Association). The group's focus was on getting parents and thier children together whom were all adopted from Latin American countries. We first met at around 15yrs old, and then reconnected May of 2013 through Facebook. From there the rest is history. Both of us being adopted made a huge impact on who we are now and why we relate on deeper level. As the saying goes, it was meant to be.
