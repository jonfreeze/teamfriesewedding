Our Story

Our love story is one of reconnecting after 20 years. In the 90's both of our parents belonged to a support group called L.A.P.A (Latin American Parent's Association). The group's focus was on getting parents and thier children together whom have been adopted from Latin America. We first met at around 15yrs old, although we knew of each other we never became close friends. Fast foward and we eventually reconnected May of 2013 through a Facebook post about John finding his birth father in Honduras. We began a long distance relationship and fell deeply in love. From there on the rest is history.
